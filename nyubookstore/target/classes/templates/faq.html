Frequently asked questions (FAQ)

Shopping

Q: Is shopping at the campus store really my best bet?

A: Yes! Your campus store works every angle to provide students with the best prices—from partnering with faculty to choose the most cost-efficient items—to offering used or rental books. Need to return or exchange a product? We make that easy for you.

Q: Why shop the campus store vs. other online retailers? 

A: With everything you need in one place, shopping the campus store means less time spent searching. Bottom line: we guarantee that you have the right course materials.

Q: How do I shop at the campus store for materials?

A: In-store systems help you quickly find what you need with course materials arranged either by course, or alphabetically by author.

Q: What’s the difference between "Textbooks" vs. "Course Materials”?

A: Course materials is a broader category that includes digital materials, and supplies like lab goggles. It's everything you need for a class.

Shipping 

Q: When will I receive my order?

A: On average, your order will be received in 7 - 10 working days. During August and January, delivery time may take longer.
